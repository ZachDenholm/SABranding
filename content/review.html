Migration Policy

Launch date: 05/03/2018

Review status: Review started

What is the review about?

​A review to examine the Migration Policy published by the Council of Ministers.

The Panel has received a number of briefings on the Migration Policy and is reviwing the underlying population trends and relevant statistics. The Panel will publish a high-level report to set out its initial observations on the Policy.

It is likely that the Migration Policy will be debated after the May 2018 elections. It is expected that further scrutiny of the detailed proposals within the policy will take place in the lead up to the States debate.

Terms of reference

  1. To undertake a high level review of the proposals laid out in R.134/2017 “Migration Policy”
  2. To identify and consider the population trends and any other relevant statistics in relation to the Migration Policy
  3. To identify any other changes or implications arising from the proposals
