Use this form to submit a topic proposal to a Scrutiny panel, submit evidence about a review or comment on a review.


Submissions, topic suggestions and comments

Any evidence you submit is carefully considered before it is made public, and we won’t publish your contact details.

All written submissions will be uploaded to the Scrutiny website. They do not suggest a panel’s endorsement or otherwise of the content. The views of a Scrutiny panel in respect of the evidence are reflected in the final report. Any evidence received under a confidential or private agreement will, in accordance with Jersey Data Protection legislation, not be released into the public domain.
